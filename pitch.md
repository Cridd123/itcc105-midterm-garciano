## Problem Statement
The old student portal is confusing because it has too many menus and is difficult to navigate. Students waste time finding important information.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The AI system helps students by providing fast and easy access to information through chat or voice. It supports users instead of replacing them.

## User Stories
- As a student, I want AI to check my clearance, so that I can save time.
- As a student, I want AI to show my grades, so that I can easily monitor my performance.
- As a student, I want AI to assist in enrollment, so that I can register quickly.
